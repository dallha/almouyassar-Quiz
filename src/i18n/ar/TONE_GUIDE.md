# Al-Mouyassar Arabic Tone Guide

## Voice
- Modern standard Arabic.
- Calm, noble, and clear.
- Warm guidance, not command-heavy.
- Spiritual-modern, not sermon-like.

## Always
- Prefer concise wording.
- Use progression language: journey, growth, steadiness, light.
- Keep UI labels short and scannable on mobile.
- Keep emotional lines gentle and breathable.

## Avoid
- Aggressive imperatives.
- Administrative or bureaucratic wording.
- Overly long poetic phrases in buttons/cards.
- Heavy formal-religious diction that slows reading.

## Microcopy Rules
- One idea per line.
- Buttons: 1-3 words when possible.
- Modal text: short sentence blocks.
- Badge titles: evocative but concise.
- Badge descriptions: action + reward context, minimal words.

## Reference Style
- Good: "واصل رحلتك"
- Good: "يتم تجهيز رحلتك..."
- Good: "سائرٌ في نور السيرة"
- Avoid: literal, rigid, or school-manual phrasing.
